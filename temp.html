Chetla Training Ceneter
In April 2017, with our partner Hope, we were able to fully fund the operating expenses of the Skills Unit in Chetla. The project provides high quality vocational training in spoken English and computer skills.

We expect to add financial accounting classes using Tally ERP-9. The students are also given communication classes so they are positive and confident during job interviews. The training equips young people with technical and social skills to be ready to join the workforce. The total number of trainees enrolled is 26 currently ranging in age 15-25 years. Staff regular conducts community visits to encourage parents to send their children to the classes.
