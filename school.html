After–School Tuition Centers
One of our greatest joys is visiting the children’s after school program in Chetla/New Alipore Basti. Over the years Varosha has supported the children by providing a healthy meal and a safe space after school. We have provided tuition to help them in school as well as spoken English, dance and art classes. This year 5 of our students appeared for and passed the West Bengal Board of Secondary Education's Madhyamik Exam. Varosha's after school tuition centers is their only academic support.
