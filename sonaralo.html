Embroidery/Fabric Printing and Painting (Sonar Alo)
On February 5th, 2011, Varosha launched this project in the village of Kumrokhali under Sonarpur –Rajpur municipality. Ladies were trained in jori, threadwork, print and coloring work on fabrics. Varosha brought the finished works of the ladies for exhibitions to Chicago. The ladies were able to get small contracts with boutiques for their works.
