The organization’s first operating principle is volunteerism. No member or volunteer will be financially compensated for their time or effort.

Money that is collected from individuals or organizations will be used, in their totality, for the benefit of the disadvantaged people whom Varosha seeks to help. Varosha is a registered not-for-profit philanthropic organization with tax exempt status under not-for-profit laws.
No members or volunteers are financially compensated for their time or effort.

Varosha will carefully and thoroughly check that its partner organizations, who implement many of Varosha’s projects, are of the highest order in their field. Varosha will seek out partner that are non-profit, philanthropic organizations that are already established in charitable endeavors. All partner organizations will be required to utilize Varosha’s resources in a manner agreed upon by Varosha, and will be required to document their expenditures.
