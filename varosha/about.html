  • About Us

Working through partners and volunteers, Varosha runs free training centers and vocational schools in some of the poorest areas of the world so that the disadvantaged people of these areas have a chance to escape poverty and make a life for themselves and their families.

From beautician school, tailoring school, Jori work, pasta making, embroidery, mushroom cultivation, aqua/poly culture to computer training, VAROSHA provides targeted and practical training to those who need it the most. Through interest free loans, Varosha helps the trained individuals create their own enterprises and become truly self-sufficient. In this regard, Varosha is uniquely positioned as a provider of “end-to-end” support for the people of the slums.

Varosha has already helped many people from poverty-stricken slums in India achieve financial independence.

  • Vision

Everyone should have the chance to provide an acceptable quality of life for themselves and their families.

We aim to actively prepare people to achieve self-sufficiency and a sustainable quality of life by providing education, resources, and expertise.

Working through partners and volunteers, Varosha runs training centers and vocational schools that train disadvantaged people in professions that will enable these people to earn an income and maintain an acceptable quality of life. Varosha seeks to lift entire communities up from poverty by running these projects on a long-term basis and with a high rate of participation within these communities.
